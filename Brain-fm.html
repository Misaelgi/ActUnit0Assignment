Tested on Brain and Behavior

Scientist at Brain.fm work with collaborators at academic insitutions to run experiments.

We look at the effects of our technology on the brain, using fMRI and EGG, and also
run large-scale behavior tests, measuringperformance on simple games.

Our experiments always include a control condition ('placebo') of the same
underlying music without Brain.fm technology applied, so we can be sure out tech is
what makes the difference.
